Field Notes

Youth Farm Mission Centre Inc. is a nonprofit organization in Rosthern, Saskatchewan, dedicated to providing Bible camps and a seasonal corn maze that bring together faith, farming, and community. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where youth and families can grow spiritually and connect with the land.

  • Rooted in the Rosthern community for generations.
  • Unique combination of faith-based camps and agritourism.
  • Hands-on farm experience for all ages.
